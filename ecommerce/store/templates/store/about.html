A little about Prólogos

We are Prólogos!

Prólogos is a bookstore that was born in 2022 with the idea of ​​offering a different experience to the user, we want to be the best bookstore in the world, we want to offer the best service and the best products, we want to be the best bookstore in the world!

Our history

We are a group of students from the UTP (Universidad Tecnológica de Pereira), who are passionate about technology and software development. We are currently in the 8th semester of our careers, and we are looking for a way to put into practice all the knowledge we have acquired throughout our careers.

We are currently developing a web application that will allow us to put into practice all the knowledge we have acquired throughout our careers. Thus, Prólogos was born!
