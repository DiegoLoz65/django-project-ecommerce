FAQ

  • What do I need to do to buy something?

    Just signup, add some books to your cart and pay. That's it.

  • How do I pay for my order?

    You need to register a credit or debit card into your account. We also manage your balance so you only need to add some cash to your account. Pretty simple, right?

  • Where can I go and pickup my order?

    You can pickup your order at any of our stores (We are currently in Colombia only). You can also choose to have your order sent to your home.

  • I didn't like the book. Can I return it?

    As long as the book is in perfect conditions, you can request a refund and we'll let you know how to proceed.

  • I'm not sure about a book. Can I reserve it?

    Yes, you can reserve a book for 24 hours. Just add it to your cart and checkout. You'll have 24 hours to pay for it.

  • I got another questions. How can I contact you?

    You can contact us by email at prologos.libreria@gmail.com
